I believe that the number one killer in the modern world is stress. Medical doctors in my church have told me that if a patient has high blood pressure, they can advise the patient to cut down on sodium. They can also advise their patients to cut down on other exesses such as sugar or cholesterol. But as doctors, there is one thing they cannot control in their patients, and that is their patients' stress levels.
